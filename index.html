Internship Program in Technology Supported Education

Research Projects

CSIR - CMERI Durgapur

IAS Summer Research Fellow • May 2013 - July 2013

When you love robotics and you have two months of free time during summer you always look for opportunities like this. I was selected as a Summer Research Fellow by the Indian Academy of Sciences(IAS) and was sent to the Robotics and Automation lab of CMERI Durgapur. There I worked under Senior Scientist Dr. Soumen Sen in a project titled "Effective tracking of moving ball using robotic arm and computer vision." I integrated a webcam to the Barret WAM Arm so that it could follow a moving ball. The video for the same can be found here. The application can be further manipulated to catch or hit a flying object.

Team AGV

Software Team Member • August 2012 - December 2013

Team AGV was formed with a vision of developing the first driverless car in India. Under the guidance of Prof. Debashish Chakravarty, it is a group of robotics enthusiasts who prefer devoting their free time to constructive research and application that to play counter strike over LAN. I worked in a small prototype of ground vehicle, Eklavya-2.0, whose main features are state estimation using GPS and IMU, PID Control, data integration and inter-process communication. It was showcased in IGVC-2013 held at Oakland University, Michigan and stood 9th among 46 teams from all around the globe.My personal contributions were towards Lane Detection(image processing), data fusion of camera and lidar, modulation using ROS
